A former top-level freestyle snowboarding athlete, then manager of a family business employing around forty people, it was after searching for meaning in my various ventures that a break in my life took place in 2019. The time has come to "do for the others" and I'm focusing my new projects on discovery, hospitality, sharing and humanitarian aid.

Over the past few years, I've made several trips to sensitive countries such as India and Nepal. I needed to be on the ground with NGOs, to understand their needs and appreciate the remarkable work they do.

So I decided to create the REJOICE association in 2024 to provide financial support to NGOs abroad.

Why abroad, you may ask, there are already so many needs in our Western countries...
What motivated my choice was the extreme urgency in which some of these organizations find themselves, fighting against a precariousness that is hard to imagine. To support children and the handicapped, who are in total destitution, abandoned by all.

Please help us by making a one-off or regular donation to the association.
I personally pledge that every euro will be donated and used to support all these wonderful human projects.

Our assistance program

Building and supporting schools

REJOICE supports schoolchildren who are taken into care to provide for their needs and education.

Disability training centers

REJOICE helps the most disadvantaged people with severe disabilities to get off the streets and become self-sufficient in a trade.

Emergency aid

REJOICE always maintains a reserve fund for emergency aid, so that we can intervene quickly if the need arises.

Orphanage support

REJOICE supports orphanages around Kathmandu. Helping to finance structures and related expenses.

* Rejoice association n° W634007916 is a non-profit association under the French law of 1901, whose purpose is to finance associations and other NGOs to carry out solidarity and charitable actions for vulnerable populations, orphans, the disabled, etc.
